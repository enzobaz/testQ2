However, while we are shown exactly how Thanos acquired five of the six stones (the Soul, Mind, Reality, Space and Time Stones), the character's pursuit of the Power Stone is only explained away in a line of dialogue.Showing up at the start of Infinity War with the Power Stone already in his possession, Thanos mentions he took hold of the stone during his attack on the planet Xandar. But we never actually see how this went down.
